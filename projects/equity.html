Systems Engineering Approach

This project demonstrated the value of systems thinking in healthcare analytics:

  • Integrated Architecture: Connected clinical data sources, operational systems, and executive dashboards into a unified analytics ecosystem
  • Scalability: Designed solutions that grow with organizational needs, from single-program tracking to multi-facility enterprise analytics
  • Real-time Insights: Implemented automated data pipelines reducing reporting latency from weeks to minutes
  • User-Centric Design: Created role-based dashboards for clinicians, administrators, and executives with appropriate complexity levels
